2026 California Festival Guide

All your festival listings for Los Angeles, San Diego, San Francisco, Sacramento, Fresno, Redding and all of California.

Welcome to California’s most comprehensive festival guide where you can find festivals for this weekend, month and year in such cities as Los Angeles, San Diego, San Francisco, Sacramento, Fresno and everywhere in-between in California.

This guide does NOT include conventions/expos, most film festivals and craft shows or events lacking the 3 essentials of a festival (food, vendors band entertainment). Feel free to contact me if you have a question about your event.

If there is a festival you do not see but would like listed, please Contact Me and let me know about it, especially smaller festivals that don’t have the advertising.

Before traveling to any festivals, please be sure to double check events via the link provided as events could have been rescheduled or cancelled without my knowledge, especially after damaging weather.

If there is an * (asterisk) next to the date, this means that I have not confirmed the date.
